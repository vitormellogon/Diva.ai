Open Datasets

Explore the open datasets we use in our projects and contribute to the advancement of AI and data science.

Why We Share Datasets

At Diva.ai, we believe in the power of open data to drive innovation and advance the field of artificial intelligence. By sharing datasets and collaborating with the broader AI community, we can collectively develop more robust models, identify new patterns, and create solutions that benefit society as a whole.

Our commitment to open data is reflected in our active participation in data sharing initiatives and our contribution to public datasets. We also leverage high-quality open datasets in our projects, ensuring that our solutions are built on diverse and representative data.

Below, you'll find a curated collection of open datasets that we've used in our projects or contributed to. We encourage you to explore these resources and leverage them for your own research and development efforts.

Retail Customer Behavior Dataset
