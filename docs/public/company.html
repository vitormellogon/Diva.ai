Our Story

From Vision to Reality

Diva.ai was founded in 2020 by Sophia Chen, a former AI Research Lead at Google, with a vision to democratize artificial intelligence and make it accessible to businesses of all sizes.

After witnessing firsthand how AI technologies were transforming large corporations but remained out of reach for smaller organizations, Sophia assembled a team of like-minded AI experts, data scientists, and business strategists to bridge this gap.

What started as a small team working out of a co-working space has now grown into a leading AI consulting firm with clients across various industries, from healthcare and finance to retail and manufacturing.

Our Headquarters

Where Innovation Happens

Diva.ai is headquartered in the heart of Silicon Valley, California, with additional offices in New York, London, and Singapore to serve our global client base.

Our state-of-the-art facilities are designed to foster collaboration, creativity, and innovation. From AI research labs to client meeting spaces, every aspect of our offices reflects our commitment to excellence and cutting-edge technology.

We've created an environment where our team can thrive, experiment with new ideas, and develop solutions that push the boundaries of what's possible with AI.
